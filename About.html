Image Steganography

Image steganography involves hiding secret data within images, a form of covert communication. It utilizes techniques to embed information in image pixels discreetly. Decoding requires specific knowledge or tools to extract the hidden data. While it offers privacy benefits, it also poses challenges in security and detection. Various applications include secure messaging, digital watermarking, and data concealment in media files.
